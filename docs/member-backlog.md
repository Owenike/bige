# Member Backlog (Recorded, Not Implemented Yet)

The following items are recorded as pending requirements and are intentionally not implemented in this batch:

- Member basic data management
  - Personal profile, contact methods, emergency contacts
- Member plan and membership status
  - Expiry date, hold/freeze, renew, upgrade/downgrade
- Class booking and cancellation
  - Group/private sessions, waitlist, attendance/no-show
- Entry QR and entry history
  - QR scan entry, historical records
- Credits/session packs
  - Remaining sessions, redemption details, expiry reminders
- Billing and payments
  - Transaction details, invoices, payment methods
- Product purchases and orders
  - Cart, order tracking, return/exchange
- Body metrics and training logs
  - Weight, body fat, workout plan
- Notification center
  - Class reminders, payment reminders, membership expiry reminders
- Customer service and tickets
  - Issue reporting and processing status
- Referrals and coupons
  - Referral code, discount coupons, campaign eligibility
- Privacy and security
  - Device management, password, permissions, consent forms

Check-in and entry exceptions are also recorded as pending:

- QR Code entry
- Check-in records (time, branch, class)
- Anomaly alerts
  - Duplicate entry, late arrival, no-show
